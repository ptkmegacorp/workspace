# Market Research - Automatic Car Wash

## Industry Overview (USA)

- **Annual Revenue:** ~$6-9 billion (US car wash industry)
- **Number of Locations:** ~15,000+ registered car washes
- **Growth Trend:** Steady 2-3% annually

## Market Drivers
- Car purchases continue to rise
- Consumer spending on convenience services
- Weather patterns (MN = lots of road salt = frequent washes)

## Typical Pricing (Midwest)
- Basic wash: $8-15
- Premium/wax: $15-25
- Unlimited monthly: $30-50

## Revenue Projections (Rough Estimates)

**Assumptions:**
- 50 cars/day average (conservative)
- Average ticket: $12
- 300 operating days/year

| Scenario | Cars/Day | Annual Revenue |

## Profit Margins
- **Gross margin:** 60-80% (low variable costs)
- **Net margin:** 15-30% after all expenses
- **ROI timeline:** 3-5 years typical

## Key Success Factors
1. Location visibility & easy access
2. Reliable equipment (Mark VII proven brand)
3. Competitive pricing
4. Weather-appropriate operation (heated bay for MN winter)
5. Payment automation (credit card/app)
