## Year 1 Projections

### Revenue (Moderate Scenario)
- 40 cars/day x 300 days x $12 avg = **$144,000**

### Expenses
| Category | Annual |
|----------|--------|
| Chemicals | $8,800 |
| Utilities | $12,000 |
| Maintenance | $7,200 |
| Insurance | $4,800 |
| CC Processing (3%) | $4,320 |
| Property tax | $3,000 |
| **Total Operating** | **$40,120** |

### Income
- Gross Revenue: $144,000
- Operating Costs: $40,120
- **Net Operating Income: $103,880**

### Debt Service (if financed $300K @ 8%, 10 yr)
- ~$44,500/year
- **Cash Flow After Debt: ~$59,380**

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low volume | Build in buffer, flexible pricing |
| Equipment breakdown | Maintenance reserve fund |
| Weather (MN winter) | Heated bay, app预订 |
| Competition | First-mover advantage |
