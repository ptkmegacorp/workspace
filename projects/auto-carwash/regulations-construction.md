# Construction & Regulations - Car Wash Facility

## Building Requirements

### Structure
- **Minimum size:** 60' x 20' (single bay) to 100' x 40' (multi-bay)
- **Clear height:** 12-14 feet minimum
- **Foundation:** Concrete, properly sloped for drainage
- **Walls:** Steel frame with metal panels or CMU block

### Zoning (Pine Island, MN)
- Check with City of Pine Island planning/zoning
- Likely requires Commercial or Industrial zoning
- Setback requirements typically 20-50 ft from property lines
- Parking requirements: 3-5 spaces per bay + stacking space

### Building Codes
- Minnesota State Building Code applies
- ADA compliance required (at least 1 accessible stall)
- Electrical: 200A+ service minimum
- Plumbing: Floor drains, water supply, sewer connection

## EPA & Environmental Regulations

### Wastewater Discharge
- **Key concern:** Soap, chemicals, oil/grease, heavy metals (if any)
- **Options:**
  1. **Municipal sewer** - Most common, requires pre-treatment
  2. **Septic system** - Not recommended for high volume
  3. **Retention pond** - Requires large space, evaporation

### Minnesota Specific
- MPCA (Minnesota Pollution Control Agency) permit likely required
- Oil/water separator required for floor drains
- pH neutralization for soap/chemical discharge
- Best Management Practices (BMPs) for stormwater

### Water Usage
- Touchless systems: 30-50 gallons/wash
- Soft-touch systems: 15-25 gallons/wash
- Recycled water systems can reduce to 8-15 gallons
- Check with city on water supply capacity

### Chemical Storage
- Secondary containment required
- SDS sheets on file
- Proper ventilation
- Chemical-resistant flooring
