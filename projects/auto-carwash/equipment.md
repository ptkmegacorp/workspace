### Feature Comparison

**Touchless (GT 300, AquaJet)**
- ✅ No brushes = no vehicle damage claims
- ✅ Lower maintenance
- ❌ Uses more water (24+ gal/wash)
- ❌ May miss stuck-on dirt

**Soft Touch (Brush-based)**
- ✅ Better cleaning quality
- ✅ Lower water usage
- ❌ Brush replacement/maintenance
- ❌ Potential damage claims

### Key Components Needed

1. **Gantry** - Moves through car, applies soap/water
2. **Dryers** - High-power blowers (10-20 HP total)
3. **Pump Station** - Pressurizes water (7.5 HP typical)
4. **Pay Station** - Credit card/cash reader
5. **Control System** - Wash cycle programming
6. **Rails** - Car guide system
